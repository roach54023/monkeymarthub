What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is a popular free online idle simulation game developed by TinyDobbins. In this charming game, you play as a monkey who runs a supermarket from scratch. You harvest crops like bananas and corn, stock the shelves, and automatically serve customers who walk into your store.

As you earn coins, you can unlock new product sections — from fresh produce to dairy and snacks — and hire helper monkeys to automate your store. The game is easy to pick up but surprisingly deep, making it perfect for both casual players and idle game enthusiasts.

How to Play Monkey Mart

Getting started with Monkey Mart is simple. Here's everything you need to know:

  • Move your monkey using WASD or the arrow keys on desktop, or the on-screen joystick on mobile.
  • Harvest crops by walking over the crop tiles — your monkey picks them up automatically.
  • Stock shelves by carrying harvested items to the corresponding shelf section.
  • Serve customers — customers walk in, pick items from shelves, and pay at the checkout. Keep shelves stocked to maximize earnings!
  • Unlock upgrades — spend your coins to unlock new sections (dairy, bakery, etc.) and hire helper monkeys.

Monkey Mart Tips & Tricks

1. Prioritize the banana section first

Bananas are your starting product and the fastest to harvest. Keep the banana shelf fully stocked at all times to maximize early-game income.

2. Hire helper monkeys as soon as possible

Helper monkeys automate harvesting and stocking, freeing you to focus on unlocking new sections. The sooner you hire them, the faster your store grows.

3. Unlock the checkout upgrade early

A faster checkout means customers spend less time waiting and you earn coins more quickly. This is one of the best early investments in the game.

4. Balance your sections

Don't over-invest in one section. Customers want variety, so unlocking multiple product types keeps a steady flow of shoppers coming in.

5. Let the game idle

Monkey Mart is an idle game — you earn coins even when you're not actively playing. Come back after a break to collect your earnings and reinvest them.
