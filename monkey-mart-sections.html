How Sections Work

Each section in Monkey Mart consists of three connected elements: a crop area where the product grows, a shelf where it's displayed for customers, and a helper slot that can be filled with an automated worker. Your main monkey (or a helper) harvests from the crop area, carries the items to the shelf, and the shelf sells to customers automatically when they walk past.

The key variables that differ between sections are harvest speed (how quickly the crop regenerates), shelf capacity (how many items fit on the shelf before it's full), and item value (how many coins each customer purchase generates). Understanding these three variables for each section is the foundation of playing Monkey Mart efficiently.

🍌 Banana Section — The Foundation

Unlock cost: Free (starting section)  |  Harvest speed: Fast  |  Item value: Low  |  Helper priority: #1 (always first)

The banana section is where every Monkey Mart run begins. You start with it unlocked and no helpers — meaning you're manually harvesting and stocking bananas from the very first second. This is intentional: the game is teaching you the core loop before giving you automation.

Bananas have the fastest harvest speed in the game. The crop regenerates quickly, which means the shelf empties quickly too — customers buy bananas faster than almost any other product. This high turnover is what makes bananas so important early on: they're your primary cash flow engine, and an empty banana shelf is the single biggest income leak in the early game.

Strategy: Your entire early-game focus should be keeping the banana shelf full while saving for your first helper monkey. The moment you can afford a helper, assign it here. Once the banana section is automated, your income becomes consistent and you can start planning your next move. Never deprioritize bananas — even in the late game with all sections running, bananas remain a significant contributor to total income due to their volume.

🌽 Corn Section — The First Expansion

Unlock cost: Early-game coins  |  Harvest speed: Medium  |  Item value: Medium  |  Helper priority: #2

Corn is the first section you unlock after bananas, and it's the moment the game starts to feel like a real supermarket. Unlocking corn brings in a new customer type — corn buyers — which increases your total customer volume and raises your income ceiling. The income jump when you unlock corn is one of the most noticeable in the game.

Corn has a medium harvest speed, which means it doesn't empty as fast as bananas but still needs regular attention. In the window between unlocking corn and assigning a helper to it, you'll be splitting your time between bananas (covered by your first helper) and corn (manual). This is manageable because your banana helper is handling the high-volume section, leaving you free to focus on corn.

Strategy: Unlock corn as your second major purchase, right after Checkout Speed Level 2. Assign your second helper to corn as soon as you can afford it. With both bananas and corn automated, your store runs largely on its own and you can start accumulating coins for the dairy unlock. Don't skip corn to go straight to dairy — the income from corn is what funds the dairy unlock efficiently.

🥛 Dairy Section — The Mid-Game Shift

Unlock cost: Mid-game coins  |  Harvest speed: Medium  |  Item value: Medium-High  |  Helper priority: #3

The dairy section is where Monkey Mart starts to feel like a late-game experience. Dairy products have higher value per item than bananas or corn, which means each customer purchase generates more coins. The income jump when you unlock dairy is significant — you'll notice your coin accumulation rate increase noticeably within a minute of opening the section.

Dairy has a medium harvest speed, similar to corn. It's not as demanding as bananas in terms of restocking frequency, but the higher value per item means an empty dairy shelf is more costly than an empty corn shelf. Prioritize keeping it stocked.

Strategy: Unlock dairy after you have helpers on both bananas and corn. At that point, your main monkey has very little to do, so manually stocking dairy for a few minutes while saving for Helper #3 is easy. Assign the helper to dairy as soon as you can — with three automated sections, your store is now generating serious passive income and the remaining unlocks come quickly.

🥚 Egg Section — The Speed Trap

Unlock cost: Mid-game coins  |  Harvest speed: Fast  |  Item value: Medium-High  |  Helper priority: #4

The egg section catches many players off guard. It has a fast harvest speed — similar to bananas — which means the shelf empties quickly and needs constant restocking. Players who unlock eggs without immediately assigning a helper often find themselves running back and forth between the egg crop and the shelf constantly, neglecting their other sections in the process.

The fast harvest speed is actually a positive once you have a helper assigned: the helper cycles quickly between harvesting and stocking, keeping the shelf consistently full without any manual effort. The egg section with a helper is one of the most efficient passive income sources in the mid-game.

Strategy: Don't unlock eggs until you're ready to assign a helper within a few minutes. The fast harvest speed makes this section particularly painful to run manually for extended periods. If you unlock it and immediately start saving for the helper, you'll be fine — just be prepared for a few minutes of active management before the helper takes over.

🍫 Chocolate Section — The Patience Test

Unlock cost: Late-game coins  |  Harvest speed: Slow  |  Item value: High  |  Helper priority: #5

Chocolate is the most helper-dependent section in the game. It has a slow harvest speed — the crop takes noticeably longer to regenerate than any other section — combined with high item value. This combination means that manually running the chocolate section is both tedious (lots of waiting at the crop) and high-stakes (each item is worth a lot, so an empty shelf is costly).

The slow harvest speed is exactly what helper monkeys are designed to handle. A helper assigned to chocolate will spend most of its time waiting at the crop for the next harvest cycle, then immediately carry the items to the shelf. This is boring work for a human player but perfectly suited to an automated helper.

Strategy: Unlock chocolate only when you can afford a helper for it within a short time. Running chocolate manually for more than a few minutes is genuinely frustrating due to the slow harvest cycle. Once the helper is assigned, chocolate becomes one of your most reliable passive income sources — high value per item, consistent output, zero manual effort required.

🍦 Ice Cream Section — The Endgame Prize

Unlock cost: Late-game coins (highest)  |  Harvest speed: Medium  |  Item value: Very High  |  Helper priority: #6 (final)

Ice cream is the premium section — the highest-value product in the game and the final unlock in the base game's progression. Unlocking it is the milestone that most players are working toward from the moment they start. The income jump when ice cream comes online is the largest single jump in the game.

Ice cream has a medium harvest speed, which makes it more manageable to run manually than chocolate. But given that it's the highest-value section, you want a helper here as soon as possible to maximize passive income. Assigning your sixth and final helper to ice cream completes your full automation setup — every section is now running on its own.

Strategy: Unlock ice cream as your final section and assign a helper immediately. At this point in the game, you should have enough passive income from your other five automated sections that saving for the ice cream helper is relatively quick. Once it's assigned, you've reached the endgame state: a fully automated store generating maximum passive income. Congratulations — you've completed Monkey Mart's main progression arc.

Sections FAQ

How many sections are in Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart has 6 sections in the base game: Banana (starting), Corn, Dairy, Egg, Chocolate, and Ice Cream. Each is unlocked progressively using coins earned from the previous sections. Monkey Mart 2 adds additional sections beyond these six.

Which Monkey Mart section makes the most money?

Ice Cream has the highest value per item and generates the most coins per customer purchase. However, Banana generates the most total income early in the game due to its fast harvest speed and high customer demand. By the late game, Ice Cream and Chocolate are your biggest earners on a per-item basis, while Banana and Egg contribute through volume.

What is the best section to assign a helper monkey to first?

Always assign your first helper monkey to the Banana section. It's your highest-volume product and the section you spend the most time manually managing. Automating it first gives you the biggest immediate income boost and frees your main monkey for everything else.

Can I unlock sections in a different order?

The section unlock order is fixed by the game — you can't skip sections. You must unlock Banana → Corn → Dairy → Egg → Chocolate → Ice Cream in that sequence. Each section requires the previous one to be unlocked first. This order is also the optimal order from a strategy perspective, so the game's structure and the optimal strategy align perfectly.

What happens after I unlock all sections?

After unlocking all six sections and assigning helpers to each, your store runs fully automatically. You've completed the main progression arc of Monkey Mart. The game continues as an idle experience — you collect coins periodically and buy remaining upgrades. Many players then move on to Monkey Mart 2 for a fresh progression loop.
