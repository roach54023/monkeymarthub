What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is a free online idle simulation game developed by TinyDobbins. You play as a monkey who builds and manages a supermarket from scratch. Harvest bananas, corn, and other crops; stock the shelves; serve customers; and hire helper monkeys to automate your store.

The game is completely free to play in any web browser — no download, no account, no payment required. Progress saves automatically in your browser's local storage.
