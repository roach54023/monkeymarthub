What's New in Monkey Mart 2?

Monkey Mart 2 builds on everything that made the original great and adds a whole new layer of depth. The store is bigger, the product range is wider, and the automation options are more powerful than ever.

New sections include a bakery, a seafood counter, and a premium goods aisle. Each section has its own unique harvest mechanic and customer type, keeping the gameplay fresh throughout.

How is Monkey Mart 2 Different from the Original?

  • More sections – The original had 6 sections; Monkey Mart 2 expands this significantly.
  • Multi-floor store – Your supermarket can now expand vertically, not just horizontally.
  • VIP customers – Special high-spending customers appear periodically for bonus earnings.
  • Advanced helper AI – Helpers in Monkey Mart 2 are smarter and more efficient.
  • New upgrade tree – More upgrade options with deeper customization for your store.

Tips for Monkey Mart 2

The same core strategies from the original apply in Monkey Mart 2, but with a few additions:

  • Prioritize unlocking the bakery section early — it has the highest customer demand.
  • VIP customers spend 3x more than regular customers. Always keep premium shelves stocked.
  • The multi-floor expansion is expensive but worth it — it doubles your store's earning potential.
  • Assign helpers to the highest-value sections first, just like in the original.
