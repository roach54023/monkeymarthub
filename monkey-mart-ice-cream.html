🍦 Monkey Mart Ice Cream Section – Complete Guide

What Is the Ice Cream Section?

The ice cream section is the sixth and final product area in Monkey Mart's base game. It sells ice cream to customers and generates the highest coins per purchase of any section in the store. Unlocking it is the culmination of the entire progression arc — every upgrade you've bought, every section you've unlocked, every helper you've assigned has been building toward this moment.

When ice cream comes online, the income jump is the largest single jump in the game. Customers who buy ice cream spend significantly more than customers buying bananas or corn. With a helper assigned and the section running automatically, ice cream becomes your biggest passive income source and the engine that funds any remaining upgrades.

How to Unlock the Ice Cream Section

The ice cream section cannot be unlocked directly — it requires completing the entire section progression in order. Here's the full unlock chain:

  • 🍌 Banana — Free, available from the start
  • 🌽 Corn — Unlock with early-game coins after banana is running
  • 🥛 Dairy — Unlock with mid-game coins after corn is running
  • 🥚 Egg — Unlock with mid-game coins after dairy is running
  • 🍫 Chocolate — Unlock with late-game coins after egg is running
  • 🍦 Ice Cream — Unlock with the highest coin cost in the game, after chocolate is running

You cannot skip sections. Ice cream is locked until chocolate is unlocked, chocolate is locked until egg is unlocked, and so on. The game enforces this order, and it's also the optimal strategic order — each section's income funds the next unlock.

The Fastest Path to Ice Cream

The speed at which you reach ice cream depends almost entirely on how efficiently you spend your coins in the earlier sections. Players who follow the optimal upgrade order reach ice cream significantly faster than those who buy randomly. Here's the condensed version of what that looks like:

Phase 1: Banana automation (minutes 0–10)

Save every coin for your first helper monkey and assign it to bananas. Don't buy anything else. Once the banana helper is running, buy Checkout Speed Level 2 to eliminate the queue that forms. These two purchases are the foundation everything else builds on.

Phase 2: Corn and dairy (minutes 10–30)

Unlock corn, assign a helper, unlock dairy, assign a helper. With three automated sections, your store is generating serious passive income. Let it idle between sessions — come back every 30–60 minutes to collect coins and buy the next upgrade.

Phase 3: Egg and chocolate (minutes 30–60)

Unlock egg, assign a helper. Unlock chocolate, assign a helper. Five automated sections means your income is compounding rapidly. The chocolate unlock is expensive, but with five helpers running, you'll accumulate the coins faster than you expect.

Phase 4: Ice cream (hour 1+)

With five sections automated and a healthy passive income stream, saving for the ice cream unlock is the final step. The unlock cost is the highest in the game, but your income at this point is also the highest it's ever been. Assign your sixth helper to ice cream immediately after unlocking it — this completes full automation and marks the end of the main progression arc.

Running the Ice Cream Section Efficiently

Ice cream has a medium harvest speed — faster than chocolate but slower than bananas or eggs. This means the shelf empties at a moderate rate, and without a helper, you'd need to restock it every few minutes. With a helper assigned, the cycle is fully automated: the helper harvests, stocks, and repeats without any input from you.

The most important thing to do after unlocking ice cream is to assign a helper immediately. Don't run it manually for more than a few minutes. The high value per item means an empty ice cream shelf is more costly than an empty shelf in any other section. Get the helper on it as fast as possible.

Once the helper is running, ice cream requires no management. It generates coins continuously, the helper keeps the shelf stocked, and customers buy it at a steady rate. Your only job is to collect the coins periodically and enjoy the passive income.

Ice Cream vs. Other Sections — How It Compares

To understand why ice cream is so valuable, it helps to compare it directly to the other sections:

vs. Banana: Bananas have much faster harvest speed but much lower value per item. Banana generates income through volume; ice cream generates income through value. In the late game, ice cream earns more per customer purchase than bananas earn in several purchases combined.

vs. Chocolate: Chocolate has higher value per item than most sections but slower harvest speed. Ice cream has comparable or higher value per item with a faster harvest cycle, making it more efficient overall. Ice cream is strictly better than chocolate in terms of income per unit of time.

vs. Dairy: Dairy is a solid mid-game section with medium value. Ice cream generates significantly more per purchase. Once ice cream is running, dairy's contribution to total income becomes relatively small by comparison.

The bottom line: ice cream is the best section in the game on a per-item basis, and its medium harvest speed means it produces that high-value output at a reasonable rate. It's not just the final unlock — it's genuinely the best earner in the store.

What Comes After Ice Cream?

After unlocking ice cream and assigning the final helper, you've completed Monkey Mart's main progression arc. The game continues as an idle experience — your store runs itself, coins accumulate, and you check in periodically to buy remaining upgrades (checkout speed levels, carry capacity).

Many players, after reaching this milestone in the base game, move on to Monkey Mart 2. The sequel expands on the original with a larger store, more sections beyond ice cream, more helper monkeys, and new mechanics. If the progression arc of the base game was satisfying, Monkey Mart 2 offers a fresh version of the same experience with more depth.

Ice Cream Section FAQ

How do you unlock the ice cream section in Monkey Mart?

The ice cream section is the final unlock in Monkey Mart's base game. You must first unlock all previous sections in order: Banana (free), Corn, Dairy, Egg, and Chocolate. Once Chocolate is unlocked and you have enough coins, the Ice Cream section becomes available to purchase. It has the highest unlock cost of any section in the game.

Is the ice cream section worth unlocking in Monkey Mart?

Yes, absolutely. Ice cream has the highest value per item of any section in the game. Unlocking it produces the largest single income jump in Monkey Mart. It's the final milestone of the base game's progression arc and the section that makes full automation most rewarding.

What is the fastest way to unlock ice cream in Monkey Mart?

The fastest path to ice cream is to follow the optimal upgrade order: buy helper monkeys for each section before unlocking the next one, keep checkout speed upgraded to prevent queues, and let the game idle between sessions to accumulate coins. Players who follow this order reach ice cream significantly faster than those who buy upgrades randomly.

Do I need a helper monkey for the ice cream section?

You don't technically need one, but you absolutely should assign one immediately after unlocking ice cream. The high value per item means an empty ice cream shelf is more costly than an empty shelf in any other section. Running it manually for more than a few minutes is a significant income loss. Assign the helper as soon as you can afford it — ideally, save for it before unlocking the section so you can assign it immediately.

Is ice cream in Monkey Mart 2 as well?

Monkey Mart 2 has its own progression with new sections. The base game's ice cream section is specific to the original Monkey Mart. Monkey Mart 2 expands the store with additional product areas beyond what the base game offers.
