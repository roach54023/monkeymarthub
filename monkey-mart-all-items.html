All Monkey Mart Sections & Items

Monkey Mart has multiple product sections, each unlocked progressively as you earn coins. Here's the complete list in unlock order:

🍌 Banana Section (Starting Section)

Unlock cost: Free (starting section)
Harvest speed: Fast
Value per item: Low
Tips: Your bread and butter. Keep this shelf stocked at all times. Assign your first helper monkey here.

🌽 Corn Section

Unlock cost: Early game coins
Harvest speed: Medium
Value per item: Medium
Tips: First unlock priority. Doubles your customer variety and income.

🥛 Dairy Section

Unlock cost: Mid-game coins
Harvest speed: Medium
Value per item: Medium-High
Tips: Good steady income. Assign a helper as soon as you unlock it.

🥚 Egg Section

Unlock cost: Mid-game coins
Harvest speed: Fast
Value per item: Medium
Tips: Fast harvest makes this easy to automate. Great for idle income.

🍫 Chocolate Section

Unlock cost: Late-game coins
Harvest speed: Slow
Value per item: High
Tips: High value but slow harvest. Must have a helper assigned to be efficient.

🍦 Ice Cream Section

Unlock cost: Late-game coins (highest)
Harvest speed: Medium
Value per item: Very High
Tips: The premium section. Unlocking this is the late-game goal. Assign your best helper here.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sections are in Monkey Mart?

There are 6 main sections: Banana, Corn, Dairy, Egg, Chocolate, and Ice Cream. Each is unlocked progressively.

What is the best section in Monkey Mart?

Ice Cream has the highest value per item. But Banana is the most important early on due to its fast harvest speed.

What is the last item to unlock in Monkey Mart?

Ice Cream is typically the final premium section to unlock in the base game.
