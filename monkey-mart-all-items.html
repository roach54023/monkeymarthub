Overview: All 6 Sections at a Glance

Monkey Mart's base game has six product sections, each selling one primary item type. They unlock in a fixed order, with each section requiring the previous one to be unlocked first. Here's the complete overview:

🍌 Section 1: Banana

The banana section is where every Monkey Mart game begins. It's free, available immediately, and sells bananas — the most basic product in the store. Bananas have the lowest value per item of any section, but they have the fastest harvest cycle, which means they generate income through volume rather than value.

Don't underestimate bananas in the late game. With a helper assigned and the section running automatically, bananas contribute a steady stream of low-value purchases that add up over time. The banana section never stops being relevant — it just becomes a background contributor rather than your primary income source.

Strategic note: Your first helper monkey should go here. The banana helper is the foundation of your passive income and the key to reaching every subsequent section faster.

🌽 Section 2: Corn

Corn is the first paid unlock in Monkey Mart. It has a fast harvest cycle similar to bananas but slightly higher value per item. Corn is a straightforward section — harvest, stock, repeat — and it's the first step in expanding your store beyond the starting banana section.

The corn section is where many players make their first strategic mistake: unlocking it before buying the banana helper. The right order is banana helper first, then corn unlock. With the banana helper running, you earn passive income that funds the corn unlock faster than you'd earn it manually.

Strategic note: Unlock corn only after the banana helper is assigned. Then save for the corn helper before unlocking dairy.

🥛 Section 3: Dairy

The dairy section sells milk and represents the mid-game transition in Monkey Mart. It has a medium harvest cycle — slower than bananas and corn, but faster than chocolate — and medium value per item. Dairy is the first section where the income-per-cycle starts to feel meaningfully higher than the early sections.

The dairy section is also where the store starts to feel busy. With three sections running (banana, corn, dairy), customers are entering the store at a higher rate and the checkout queue can start to form. This is a good time to buy Checkout Speed Level 3 if you haven't already.

Strategic note: After assigning the dairy helper, check your checkout speed. If queues are forming, buy the next checkout speed upgrade before saving for the egg unlock.

🥚 Section 4: Egg

The egg section sells eggs and has a fast harvest cycle with medium-to-high value per item. It's one of the more efficient sections in the game — fast production combined with decent value means it generates income at a good rate. The egg section is often where players start to feel the power of full automation, with three or four helpers running simultaneously.

Eggs are also notable for being the section that unlocks chocolate. Once the egg helper is assigned and the section is running smoothly, saving for the chocolate unlock is the next priority.

Strategic note: The egg section is a good income booster. Assign the helper promptly and let it run while you save for chocolate.

🍫 Section 5: Chocolate

Chocolate is the penultimate section and the first one with a slow harvest cycle. Chocolate takes longer to produce than any other item in the game, but it has high value per item — the slow cycle is compensated by the high price customers pay for it. This makes chocolate a high-value, low-frequency income source.

The slow harvest cycle means the chocolate helper spends more time waiting between cycles than helpers in other sections. This is normal — don't reassign the helper thinking it's not working. The slow cycle is a feature of the section, not a bug. The high value per item makes it worthwhile.

Strategic note: Chocolate is the gateway to ice cream. Assign the helper, let it run, and save for the ice cream unlock. Don't buy any other upgrades until ice cream is unlocked.

🍦 Section 6: Ice Cream

Ice cream is the final section and the crown jewel of Monkey Mart. It has the highest value per item of any product in the game and a medium harvest cycle — faster than chocolate but slower than bananas. This combination makes it the most efficient income source in the store: high value produced at a reasonable rate.

Unlocking ice cream produces the largest single income jump in the game. When the ice cream section comes online and the helper is assigned, your total store income increases dramatically. This is the moment the game's progression arc reaches its conclusion.

Strategic note: Assign the sixth helper to ice cream immediately after unlocking it. Don't run it manually for more than a few minutes — the high value per item means an empty ice cream shelf is more costly than an empty shelf in any other section.

How Items Work: The Mechanics

Every item in Monkey Mart follows the same mechanical pattern. A growing or production station generates items on a cycle. When the cycle completes, items are available to harvest. Your monkey (or a helper) walks to the station, collects the items, walks to the corresponding shelf, and stocks it. Customers then walk to the shelf, pick up items, and carry them to the checkout. After paying, coins appear on the floor.

The key variables that differ between sections are harvest speed (how fast the station produces items), value per item (how many coins each customer purchase generates), and shelf capacity (how many items the shelf can hold before it's full). These variables determine each section's income rate and how much attention it needs.

Items in Monkey Mart 2

If you've completed the base game and want more items to manage, Monkey Mart 2 expands the product lineup significantly. The sequel introduces new sections beyond ice cream, more helper monkeys, and a larger store layout. It's the natural next step for players who've mastered the base game's six sections.

All Items FAQ

How many items are in Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart's base game has 6 product sections: Banana, Corn, Dairy, Egg, Chocolate, and Ice Cream. Each section sells one primary product type. The sections are unlocked in order, with banana available from the start and ice cream being the final unlock.

What is the most valuable item in Monkey Mart?

Ice cream is the most valuable item in Monkey Mart's base game. It has the highest coins-per-purchase of any product in the store. This is why unlocking the ice cream section produces the largest single income jump in the game.

What items does Monkey Mart sell?

Monkey Mart sells bananas, corn, dairy products (milk), eggs, chocolate, and ice cream. Each product comes from a dedicated growing or production station in the store and is sold from a dedicated shelf. Customers automatically pick items from shelves and pay at the checkout.

Which item has the fastest harvest speed in Monkey Mart?

Bananas and eggs have the fastest harvest cycles in the game. Corn is also fast. Dairy and ice cream have medium harvest speeds. Chocolate has the slowest harvest cycle of any section.

Are there more items in Monkey Mart 2?

Yes, Monkey Mart 2 introduces new product sections beyond the six in the base game. The sequel expands the store with additional items, more helper monkeys, and new mechanics. It's designed for players who've completed the base game's progression arc.
