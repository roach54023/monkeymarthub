📖 How to Play Monkey Mart – Complete Guide

Everything you need to know to master Monkey Mart, from basic controls to advanced upgrade strategies. Updated for 2026.

Monkey Mart Overview

Monkey Mart is an idle supermarket management game where you control a monkey who runs their own store. Your job is to harvest products, keep shelves stocked, and serve customers — all while expanding your store and hiring helpers to automate the process.

The game starts simple but grows into a satisfying idle experience as you unlock new sections and automate more of your store's operations.

Controls

⌨️

Desktop

WASD or Arrow Keys to move your monkey in any direction.

📱

Mobile / Tablet

Use the on-screen joystick in the bottom-left corner to move.

🖱️

Mouse (optional)

Some actions can be triggered by clicking, but keyboard/joystick is recommended.

Step-by-Step: Your First 5 Minutes

Step 1 – Harvest Your First Crop

When you start, you'll see a banana plant in the top section of your store. Walk your monkey over it and you'll automatically pick up bananas. Your monkey can carry several items at once.

Step 2 – Stock the Banana Shelf

After harvesting, walk to the banana shelf (marked with a banana icon). Your monkey will automatically place the bananas on the shelf. Customers will now be able to buy them.

Step 3 – Watch Customers Come In

Customers enter from the left side of the store. They walk to a shelf, pick up an item, and head to the checkout counter on the right. You earn coins for each sale. The more stocked your shelves are, the more customers you can serve.

Step 4 – Keep Cycling

Your main job in the early game is to keep cycling between the crop area and the shelves. Harvest → Stock → Repeat. As you earn more coins, you can start upgrading.

Step 5 – Buy Your First Upgrade

Click on any locked section or upgrade icon to see what's available. The first upgrade to buy is usually the Corn section or a helper monkey. Helper monkeys automate harvesting so you can focus on stocking.

Understanding the Store Layout

Your supermarket is divided into sections, each selling a different product:

  • 🍌 Banana Section – Starting section, fast to harvest
  • 🌽 Corn Section – First unlock, higher value
  • 🥛 Dairy Section – Mid-game unlock, good income
  • 🍫 Chocolate Section – Late-game, high value
  • 🥚 Egg Section – Steady income, easy to automate
  • 🍦 Ice Cream Section – Premium section, top earnings

Each section has its own crop area, shelf, and can be assigned a helper monkey.

Upgrades Explained

Helper Monkeys

Helper monkeys are the most important upgrade in the game. Each helper is assigned to a specific section and will automatically harvest crops and stock shelves for that section. Once you have helpers covering all sections, your store runs on autopilot.

Checkout Speed

Upgrading the checkout counter reduces the time each customer spends paying. This directly increases your coins-per-minute, making it one of the best value upgrades in the game.

Carry Capacity

Increasing your monkey's carry capacity means fewer trips between the crop area and the shelves. This is especially useful in the early game before you have helpers.

Section Unlocks

Each new section you unlock adds a new product type, attracting more customers and increasing your total earning potential. Unlock sections in order — don't skip ahead, as each section builds on the previous one's income.

Idle Mechanics – Earning While Away

Monkey Mart is an idle game, which means your store continues to earn coins even when you're not actively playing — as long as you have helper monkeys assigned. When you come back after a break, you'll find a pile of coins waiting for you.

The longer you leave the game idle, the more you earn. This makes Monkey Mart perfect for playing in short sessions throughout the day.

Common Questions

Why aren't customers coming to my store?

Customers only visit shelves that have stock. If your shelves are empty, customers will leave without buying. Make sure to keep all shelves stocked, especially in the early game.

How do I get more coins fast?

The fastest way to earn coins is to keep all shelves fully stocked at all times and upgrade the checkout speed. Also, unlock new sections as soon as you can afford them — each new section brings in more customers.

What should I upgrade first?

Priority order: 1) Hire your first helper monkey, 2) Upgrade checkout speed, 3) Unlock the corn section, 4) Hire a second helper monkey. This gives you the best early-game income growth.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes. Clear your browser's local storage for this site to reset the game. Note that this will permanently delete all your progress.
