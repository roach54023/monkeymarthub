How to Play Monkey Mart

If you're new to Monkey Mart, here's the quick version of how it works. You control a monkey who runs a supermarket. You start with a single banana section — walk to the banana crop, harvest bananas, carry them to the shelf, and serve customers at the checkout. As you earn coins, you unlock new product sections and hire helper monkeys to automate each one.

The game's arc is a satisfying progression from doing everything manually to watching a fully automated store run itself. The key insight is that helper monkeys are always the highest-priority upgrade — buy your first one as soon as possible and assign it to the banana section. Everything else follows from there.

For a complete breakdown of what to buy and when, see the Monkey Mart Upgrade Guide. For a stage-by-stage walkthrough of the entire game, see the Monkey Mart Walkthrough.

Controls

  • Desktop: WASD or arrow keys to move your monkey around the store.
  • Mobile / Chromebook touch: On-screen joystick in the bottom-left corner.
  • Harvesting: Walk over the crop area — your monkey picks up items automatically on contact.
  • Stocking shelves: Walk to the shelf with items in hand — stocking happens automatically.
  • Checkout: Walk to the checkout counter when customers are queuing.
  • Upgrades: Tap the upgrade/shop button to open the purchase menu.
