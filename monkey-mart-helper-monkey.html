🐒 Monkey Mart Helper Monkey – Complete Guide

Helper monkeys are the single most important mechanic in Monkey Mart. They turn your store from a manual grind into a passive income machine. Here's everything you need to know: how to get them, where to assign them, and how to use them to reach full automation.

What Does a Helper Monkey Do?

A helper monkey is a staff member for one specific section of your supermarket. When assigned to a section, it takes over two tasks that you would otherwise have to do manually:

Harvesting: The helper walks to the crop area for its section and picks up items automatically, on a continuous loop. You never need to harvest that crop yourself again.

Shelf stocking: After harvesting, the helper carries the items to the shelf and stocks it. When the shelf runs low, the helper goes back to harvest more. This cycle repeats indefinitely without any input from you.

The result is that a section with a helper assigned runs completely on autopilot. Customers come in, pick items off the shelf, and the helper keeps it stocked. Your main monkey is freed up entirely from that section.

How to Get a Helper Monkey

Helper monkeys are purchased from the upgrade shop, which you access by tapping the upgrade button (usually a wrench or coin icon) during gameplay. The helper monkey upgrade appears in the shop once you have at least one section available to assign it to.

The cost of each helper monkey increases with each one you buy — your first helper is the cheapest, and each subsequent one costs more. This is intentional: the game is designed so that your income grows fast enough to keep up with the increasing costs, as long as you're following a reasonable upgrade order.

After purchasing a helper monkey, you'll be prompted to choose which section to assign it to. You can only assign one helper per section, and you cannot reassign a helper once it's placed (in the base game). Choose carefully — though in practice, the correct assignment is almost always the newest section you just unlocked.

Which Section to Assign Each Helper

The assignment order follows the section unlock order almost exactly. Here's the recommended assignment for each helper:

Helper #1 → Banana Section

This is non-negotiable. Bananas are your starting product, your highest-volume item, and the section you spend the most time manually managing at the start. Automating it first gives you the biggest immediate quality-of-life improvement and the highest income boost. Your main monkey is now free to handle everything else.

Helper #2 → Corn Section

Assign your second helper to corn as soon as you unlock the corn section (or shortly after). Corn has medium harvest speed and medium value — it's not as fast as bananas, but it brings in a new customer type that increases your total customer volume. Automating it lets you focus on the next section.

Helper #3 → Dairy Section

Dairy has higher value per item than corn, which means the helper here generates more coins per cycle than the corn helper. Assign it to dairy as soon as you unlock that section. With three helpers running, your store is now largely self-sufficient and you can start leaving it idle for longer periods.

Helper #4 → Egg Section

Eggs have a fast harvest speed, which means the shelf empties quickly. Without a helper, you'll find yourself constantly running back to restock eggs. Assigning a helper here eliminates that friction and keeps the shelf full without any effort from you.

Helper #5 → Chocolate Section

Chocolate has a slow harvest speed but high value per item. This combination makes it the section that benefits most from a helper — the slow harvest means you'd spend a lot of time waiting at the crop if you were doing it manually. A helper handles the slow harvest cycle automatically while you do other things.

Helper #6 → Ice Cream Section

Ice cream is the highest-value section in the game. Assigning your final helper here completes your full automation setup. At this point, every section runs itself, and your main monkey has nothing mandatory to do. You've reached the endgame state of Monkey Mart.

What Happens After Full Automation

Once all 6 helpers are assigned, Monkey Mart becomes a true idle game. Your store generates coins continuously without any input. The only things left to do are:

  • Collect coins periodically (there's an offline earnings cap, so check in every hour or two)
  • Upgrade checkout speed if there's still a queue forming
  • Buy any remaining carry capacity upgrades (low priority at this point)
  • Explore Monkey Mart 2 for a fresh progression loop with new sections and mechanics

Full automation is the goal the entire game is designed around. Every upgrade decision before this point should be evaluated by one question: does this get me to full automation faster? Helper monkeys are always the answer.

Common Helper Monkey Mistakes

Mistake 1: Buying a helper for the wrong section

Some players assign their first helper to a section other than bananas — usually because they just unlocked a new section and want to automate it immediately. This is a mistake. Bananas are your highest-volume product and the section you spend the most time on. Always automate bananas first, then work through the sections in unlock order.

Mistake 2: Delaying the first helper to buy other upgrades

Checkout speed and carry capacity upgrades are tempting because they're cheap and feel impactful. But delaying your first helper by even a few minutes costs you significant income over the course of a session. The helper pays for itself very quickly — buy it first, always.

Mistake 3: Unlocking sections without a helper plan

Unlocking a new section before you can afford a helper for it means you're manually stocking that section indefinitely. This is fine for a minute or two, but if you unlock multiple sections without helpers, you'll find yourself running between sections constantly and never having enough time to keep everything stocked. Unlock one section, assign a helper, then unlock the next.

Helper Monkey FAQ

How do you get a helper monkey in Monkey Mart?

Helper monkeys are purchased from the upgrade shop using coins. They appear as an upgrade option once you have a section to assign them to. Tap or click the upgrade button, select the helper monkey upgrade, and then choose which section to assign it to. The first helper is the cheapest; each subsequent one costs more.

How many helper monkeys can you have in Monkey Mart?

You can have one helper monkey per section. Since Monkey Mart has 6 sections (Banana, Corn, Dairy, Egg, Chocolate, Ice Cream), the maximum is 6 helper monkeys — one fully automating each section. With all 6 assigned, your entire store runs on autopilot.

Which section should I assign my first helper monkey to?

Always assign your first helper monkey to the Banana section. Bananas are your starting product and highest-volume item. Automating this section first frees your main monkey to handle everything else and provides the biggest immediate income boost. This is the single most impactful decision in the early game.

What does a helper monkey do exactly?

A helper monkey assigned to a section automatically harvests the crop for that section and restocks the shelf when it runs low. It works continuously without any input from you, effectively running that section on autopilot. You never need to manually harvest or stock that section again.

Can you reassign a helper monkey to a different section?

In the base game, helper monkeys cannot be reassigned once placed. This is why the assignment order matters — always assign to the section you most need automated right now, which is almost always the newest section you just unlocked (with the exception of the first helper, which always goes to bananas).
